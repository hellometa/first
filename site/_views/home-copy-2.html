welcome to meta5

meta5 unites all of your data assets within one easy-to-use platform. It integrates with the programs and applications you already use, without writing a single line of code—driving more opportunities to leverage and gather insights for business intelligence.

our difference

meta5 makes the decision-making process easier, more productive, cost-effective and scalable vs. traditional stand-alone Business Intelligence (BI) tools.
seamless

Seamless Data Integration

meta5 leverages your existing end-user software and application environments so your team can compile data from disparate resources—and create effective reports within minutes.
excellence

Microsoft Excel Superiority

meta5 is simply the best Excel integration and Office automation tool in the industry. We eliminate the cutting and pasting typically required to reconstruct your sophisticated Excel and Word templates, and deliver the power of macros without writing any code.
governance

Reporting

meta5 automatically audits, manages, and documents your data—so you can feel confident in reproducing and tracking any fact on any report to its source.
flexible

Efficient and Flexible Reporting

meta5 delivers superior business process automation that manages, creates and delivers reports five times faster than other code-based solutions, and makes it easy to update and modify them at will to create even more compelling insights and analysis.
code free

Code and Macro Free

meta5’s CAPSULE® Technology helps build reports via clear, simple visual icons, giving your data analysts and account managers the power of being a programmer without the need to write code.

the meta5 process

From integrating and combining data from disparate sources to automating, managing, auditing and distributing reports, meta5 helps you do it all.

strategy

Control any kind of data from anywhere, so you spend less time managing it—and more time making strategic decisions from it.

time

Automate the time-consuming transformation of data needed for reporting and distribution, getting the job done in a fraction of the time.

simple

Create your own custom applications using our simple drag-and-drop interface and our patented CAPSULE® technology.

insight

Share insights across teams to accelerate your business KPIs, ROIs and bottom line from end-to-end.
